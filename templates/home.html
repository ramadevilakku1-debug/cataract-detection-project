Cataract Identification Overview

Cataract is one of the leading causes of visual impairment globally, characterized by clouding of the eye’s natural lens, which results in blurry or distorted vision. Early detection is crucial for timely treatment and preventing permanent vision loss.

In this project, we leverage cutting-edge Vision Transformer (ViT) architecture to automatically identify cataracts from eye images. By harnessing deep learning and attention mechanisms, our model learns to focus on critical regions of the eye, achieving high accuracy in classification.

This automated approach enhances diagnostic efficiency, supports ophthalmologists, and improves accessibility to early eye disease detection, especially in remote or underserved areas.
